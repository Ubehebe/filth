filth
modern http

filth is an extensible C++ framework for building high-performance clients and servers. In particular, those that speak the Hypertext Transfer Protocol. In particular, servers, caches, and proxies conforming to the HTTP/1.1 standard.

filth was written by Brendan Linn in the spring of 2010 for fun. It's available under the terms of the GNU General Public License.
